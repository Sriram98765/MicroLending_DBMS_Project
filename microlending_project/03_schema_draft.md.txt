# Relational Schema Draft

## 1. Customers
* `customer_id` (INT, PK, Auto-Inc)
* `first_name`, `last_name` (VARCHAR)
* `gov_id` (VARCHAR, Unique)
* `email` (VARCHAR, Unique)
* `dob` (DATE)

## 2. LoanApplications
* `application_id` (INT, PK, Auto-Inc)
* `customer_id` (INT, FK -> Customers)
* `amount_requested` (DECIMAL)
* `status` (ENUM: Pending, Approved, Rejected)

## 3. Loans
* `loan_id` (INT, PK, Auto-Inc)
* `application_id` (INT, FK -> LoanApplications)
* `principal_amount` (DECIMAL)
* `interest_rate` (DECIMAL)
* `outstanding_balance` (DECIMAL)
* `status` (ENUM: Active, Paid_Off, Defaulted)

## 4. Schedule
* `schedule_id` (INT, PK)
* `loan_id` (INT, FK -> Loans)
* `due_date` (DATE)
* `installment_amount` (DECIMAL)
* `is_paid` (BOOLEAN)

## 5. Repayments
* `repayment_id` (INT, PK)
* `loan_id` (INT, FK -> Loans)
* `amount_paid` (DECIMAL)
* `payment_date` (TIMESTAMP)

## 6. Users & Roles
* `Roles`: `role_id` (PK), `role_name`
* `Users`: `user_id` (PK), `username`, `password_hash`, `role_id` (FK)
